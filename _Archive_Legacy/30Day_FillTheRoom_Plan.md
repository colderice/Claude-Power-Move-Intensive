# 30-Day Fill The Room Plan
## Claude Power Move Intensive | Atlanta, May 23 | 40 Seats | Start: April 12

---

## THE MATH

40 seats to sell in 30 days = 1.33 seats per day.
That's not hard. That's a sequencing problem, not a sales problem.

**Phase 1 — Launch (Days 1-7):** Build the engine. 5-8 seats.
**Phase 2 — Momentum (Days 8-14):** Pour fuel on it. 10-15 seats.
**Phase 3 — Early Bird Blitz (Days 15-20):** Create the deadline. 10-12 seats.
**Phase 4 — Final Close (Days 21-30):** Scarcity and personal outreach. Last 8-10 seats.

---

## BEFORE DAY 1 — DO THIS FIRST

Before you do anything else, create the funnel architecture. Right now you have one product (the event ticket). That's a dead end. Here's what you need:

**Front Door:** A low-ticket "Claude Power Move Intensive Starter Kit" — $27 to $17. A PDF or short video that introduces Eva, Penny, Sonny, and Stan. Sells itself. Gets buyers in the database. Add a bump: "Upgrade to the Claude Power Move Intensive at the early bird rate."

**Continuity:** AI Profit Lab is your post-event offer. Every attendee gets a free 30-day trial. It converts the room into monthly revenue.

**Back End:** TSP Mastermind. Pitch it from stage or in the VIP room. The event is the front end. The Mastermind is where the real money is.

Build the Starter Kit this weekend. One PDF, one sales page, one order bump. 4 hours of work. Do it before Day 1.
